Features

Everything You Need to Keep Your Sites Online

Powerful monitoring tools, deep diagnostics, and instant alerts — built for developers who care about uptime.

Real-Time Monitoring

Check your websites every 1–30 minutes. Get instant status updates with response time tracking and uptime percentage calculations.

Instant Alerts

Receive email notifications the moment your site goes down or recovers. Configurable alert thresholds to avoid false positives.

SSL & Security Audit

Automatic checks for HTTPS, HSTS, CSP, X-Frame-Options, and X-Content-Type-Options. Know your security posture instantly.

Performance Grading

A+ to F grades based on response time, DNS lookup, and connection speed. Identify bottlenecks before they become problems.

Infrastructure Detection

Automatically detect CDN providers (Cloudflare, Vercel, AWS), web servers (Nginx, Apache), and technologies (React, WordPress, Next.js).

Rich Analytics

Visual uptime history bars, response time charts with time-range selection, redirect chain tracking, and detailed timing breakdowns.

Deep Diagnostics

5-tab diagnostic panel: Overview, Timing (DNS/Connect/Download), Security, Infrastructure, and Raw data with full response headers.

Privacy Focused

Your data is securely stored and never shared. Enjoy complete control over your monitoring data with enterprise-grade security.
